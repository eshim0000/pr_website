Who We Are

We are pioneers of autonomous robotics for precision lawn weeding.

While our immediate focus is on revolutionizing lawn weeding through precision robotics, our vision extends to the broader transformation of grounds operations. We're driven by a commitment to environmental stewardship, starting with dramatically reducing chemical usage in lawn care.

Conventional lawn management relies heavily on chemicals that impact our environment and communities. Meanwhile, organic alternatives often require intensive manual labor, making them impractical for widespread adoption.

Our autonomous robotics platform bridges this gap, beginning with lawn care and eventually expanding to comprehensive grounds management. By automating labor-intensive tasks like weeding, we're making sustainable lawn maintenance accessible today while building toward a future of fully automated grounds operations.
