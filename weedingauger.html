F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

May I use my home drill?

RootGone Weeding Auger Drill Bit may be used with typical home drills with chuck size 3/8 inch or larger.

Why do I need a side handle for the drill?

A side handle for the drill reduces the risk of injury dramatically when a kickback occurs.

Is a drill included?

No, a drill is not included in the product.

How do I remove tangled plants from the product?

When the drill does not rotate, you can remove the tangled plants using a garden scissor easily. You can cut the tangled plants with a garden scissor and take them out by hand.
